Cloud backup destinations — 4 design options

Same scenario in each: AWS Production with EC2/S3/RDS/Lambda workloads + 2 Gateways. Backup goes to Native S3 (Standard 80 TB · Glacier 200 TB) AND Air Gap Protect (Hot 50 TB · Cool 150 TB). Each option shows the BOTH case as the headline, with mini "Native-only" / "AGP-only" variants below. Compare and pick.

Option 1 — Two parallel cards, side by side

parallel structure, equal weight, easy scan takes horizontal width

Two equal-size sibling cards tucked under the Command-Center / Gateway row. AWS-orange border on the native card, purple on the AGP card. When only one destination exists, render only its card.
